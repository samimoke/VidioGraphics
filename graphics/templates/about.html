About School of Omars

WHo we are?

Our Missions

Photography and Videography Training Institute, Well Recognised by the Oromia Employment Bureau, and is currently providing Vocational Training in a very prophetic manner in both its branches in Burayu and Ambo.

Our Vision

To empower students with technical mastery faster their creative expression, and prepare them for professional success.

Formed in 2012 by Hambasan and His Friends, Omars is an award-winning, full-service production company specializing in Videography, Graphics Design, Motion Graphics, Acting, Directing, Screen play writting & Multimedia journalism and Video production services has been featured.
